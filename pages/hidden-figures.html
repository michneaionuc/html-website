Hidden Figures

Back to List

Director: Theodore Melfi

Based on the true story of three African American women—Katherine Johnson, Dorothy Vaughan, and Mary Jackson—who worked as mathematicians at NASA during the 1960s.
The film highlights their critical contributions to the success of the U.S. space program, particularly John Glenn's orbital mission, while they faced racial and gender discrimination.
Celebrating their brilliance and perseverance, Hidden Figures showcases their fight for equality and recognition in a predominantly white and male workplace.
