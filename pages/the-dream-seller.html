The Dreamseller

Director: Jayme Monjardim

After a beggar saves a depressed psychologist from suicide, they develop a unique friendship that helps both of them gain strength and confront life's challenges more effectively.
The story illustrates that while dreams and aspirations are essential for motivation and hope, relying solely on them can lead to disillusionment and an evasion of real-life obstacles.
Ultimately, the film emphasizes that true fulfillment arises not from fleeting dreams but from facing one's reality and taking concrete steps toward achieving personal goals.
