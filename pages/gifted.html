Gifted

Director: Marc Webb

The film follows Frank Adler (played by Chris Evans) as he raises his gifted niece, Mary (portrayed by McKenna Grace), after the tragic death of her mother. Mary exhibits extraordinary mathematical talent at a remarkably young age.
As Frank strives to give her a normal childhood, he faces a challenging legal battle against her estranged mother's family, who believe that Mary should be placed in a more rigorous academic environment. The film delves into profound themes of love, family dynamics, and the pressures of exceptional intelligence, ultimately emphasizing the importance of prioritizing a child's happiness over societal expectations.
