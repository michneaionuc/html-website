The Shawshank Redemption

Director: Jayme Monjardim

Andy Dufresne, a successful banker, is wrongfully convicted for the murders of his wife and her lover, resulting in a life sentence at Shawshank Prison. He soon becomes the most unconventional prisoner, defying the odds.
The film underscores the significance of maintaining hope, resilience, and friendship, illustrating that true freedom comes from within, regardless of external challenges.
